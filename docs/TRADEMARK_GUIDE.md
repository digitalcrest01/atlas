# Myatlastic — Trademark Filing Guide

**Project Atlastic · Brand protection**

You have confirmed via your own search that "Myatlastic" is currently unregistered in your target markets. This guide covers the steps to file in the UK, US, and EU.

---

## Why trademark now

- App Store and Play Store listings are public — competitors can see your brand name the moment you submit
- Filing establishes a priority date even before grant — any later filer is secondary to your date
- Trademark registration takes 4-12 months; file now so you're protected by launch +6 months
- Without registration, enforcement of the brand name is possible under common law (unregistered TM) but expensive and uncertain

---

## What to protect

| Mark type | What you're filing |
|---|---|
| Word mark | `MYATLASTIC` (the word, any font) |
| Optional | Logo mark (the compass-star + word) — file separately if budget allows |

A word mark is broader and more valuable than a logo mark. File the word mark first.

## Classes to register in (Nice Classification)

| Class | Description | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **9** | Computer software, mobile applications, downloadable apps, educational software | The app itself |
| **41** | Education, training, entertainment, online educational services | The quiz/learning service |
| **42** | Software as a service (SaaS) | The web app / subscription |

Filing all three classes increases cost but protects comprehensively. Minimum recommendation: **Class 9 + Class 41**.

## PART 1 — United Kingdom (UKIPO)

**Authority**: Intellectual Property Office (UKIPO)  
**URL**: https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-a-trademark  
**Cost**: £170 for first class + £50 per additional class  
**Timeline**: ~4 months to registration  
**Examiner objection period**: 2 months after publication

### Steps

1. Go to https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-a-trademark
2. Create a UKIPO account (or use the guest flow)
3. Start the online application ("Right Start" — includes a preliminary examination for an extra £100; worth it to catch issues early)
4. Enter mark: `MYATLASTIC` (plain text, no stylisation)
5. Select classes: 9 and 41 (add 42 optionally)
6. Describe goods/services:
   - Class 9: "Downloadable mobile application software for educational purposes relating to world geography; computer software for providing access to an online database of country information."
   - Class 41: "Education services provided via an app; online interactive educational games; provision of quizzes and daily challenges relating to world geography."
7. State your address (or a UK agent's address — not required)
8. Pay online: £170 (Class 9) + £50 (Class 41) = **£220 total** (or £270 with Right Start)
9. You will receive a filing receipt with your application number and priority date

Post-filing:
- Examination: ~1-2 months
- Publication: ~2 months after acceptance
- Opposition period: 2 months
- Registration: ~1 month after opposition period closes

**Priority date** is set on the day you file. Keep your receipt.

## PART 2 — United States (USPTO)

**Authority**: United States Patent and Trademark Office  
**URL**: https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/apply  
**Cost**: $250 per class (TEAS Plus) or $350 per class (TEAS Standard)  
**Timeline**: 12-18 months (significant backlog)

### Steps

1. Go to https://teas.uspto.gov (Trademark Electronic Application System)
2. Create or log into your USPTO account
3. Select **TEAS Plus** (cheaper, requires using pre-defined ID Manual descriptions)
4. Enter mark: `MYATLASTIC` — select "Standard characters" (word mark only)
5. Owner: Your legal name or company name and US mailing address (non-US applicants may file but must appoint a US-licensed attorney to respond to any Office Actions — consider using a service like Trademark Engine, LegalZoom, or a US IP attorney)
6. Basis: **Section 1(b)** "Intent to Use" if the app isn't live in the US yet; or **Section 1(a)** "Use in Commerce" if it is
7. Describe goods/services:
   - Class 009: "Downloadable mobile applications for use in geography education"
   - Class 041: "Educational services, namely providing online interactive games and quizzes featuring world geography"
8. Pay: $250 × 2 classes = **$500 total**

**Important for US**: If you file 1(b) (intent to use), you have 6 months from Notice of Allowance to file a Statement of Use (showing the app is live in the US). Extensions cost $125/class per 6-month period, up to 3 years total.

**Recommended approach**: Use a service like Trademark Engine (~$299 + USPTO fees) or an IP attorney for the US filing due to its complexity. Budget $800-$1,200 total for US.

## PART 3 — European Union (EUIPO)

**Authority**: European Union Intellectual Property Office  
**URL**: https://euipo.europa.eu/eSearch/#basic  
**Cost**: €850 for first class + €50 per additional class (one-time fee, covers all 27 EU member states)  
**Timeline**: ~4-6 months (if no opposition)

### Steps

1. Search first at https://euipo.europa.eu/eSearch/#basic to confirm availability
2. Go to https://euipo.europa.eu/ohimportal/en/apply-now
3. Select "European Union Trade Mark (EUTM)"
4. Enter the mark: `MYATLASTIC`
5. Type: "Word mark"
6. Language: English (first language); choose a second EUIPO language (Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese — pick one)
7. Applicant: your name/company + address
8. Classes: 9 and 41 (add 42 for €50 more)
9. Describe goods/services using EUIPO's Harmonised Database (HDB) — search for "mobile application" and "educational software" terms
10. Pay: €850 + €50 = **€900 total** for 2 classes

**Note**: EUTM protection does NOT cover Switzerland, Norway, or the UK (post-Brexit). File UK separately (already done above) and add Switzerland later if needed.

## Priority strategy — file in this order

1. **UK first** — cheapest, fastest, establishes EU/US priority date if you file the others within 6 months (Paris Convention)
2. **EU second** — within 6 months of UK filing to claim Paris Convention priority
3. **US third** — within 6 months of UK filing; or file independently

Filing UK first and then claiming priority in the EU and US within 6 months means all three filings get the **UK filing date** as their effective priority date.

**Paris Convention priority period: 6 months.** Don't miss this window.

## Watch services (post-filing)

Once filed, you should monitor for conflicting applications. Free tools:
- **UK**: UKIPO's free "Trade Mark Journal" email alerts
- **EU**: EUIPO's "Trade Marks Watch" service (free basic tier)
- **US**: USPTO's TESS database (manual search); paid services: TrademarkNow, Corsearch

---

## Using a trademark attorney

Recommended for the US application due to the complexity of Office Actions. For UK and EU, self-filing via the official online portals is straightforward and saves £200-£400 in agent fees per filing.

UK IP attorneys who do flat-rate filings: Marks & Clerk, Withers & Rogers, HGF Law. Expect £300-£600 + UKIPO fees.

## After registration

- Add the ® symbol in all marketing materials (UK, EU, US once registered — NOT before)
- Use ™ during the application period
- Renew: UK every 10 years (£200), EU every 10 years (€1,000), US every 5/10 years (~$325)

---

*Filing deadlines are absolute. Paris Convention priority window is exactly 6 months from first filing date. Calendar it immediately.*
